President

Hello guys, my name is Dheeraj Tallapragada, and I am the co president of the DHS Table Tennis Club! I started playing table tennis for fun as many people did as well. However, I found an unexpected interest for the sport. I started playing numerous tournaments and gained a decently high rating of about 1300. I felt that this sport was not as recognized in the U.S. as it should be, so my goal as co president of this club is to make this sport more well known by the students of DHS.
