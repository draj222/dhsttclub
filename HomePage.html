About the Club

Hello everyone, and welcome to the official DHS Table Tennis Club Website! Table Tennis is a highly unpopular sport in the U.S. and our mission is to change that because we truly believe that Table Tennis deserves to be recognized. By creating this club, we are giving an opportunity for all DHS students to not only play Table Tennis, but to learn the fundamentals and implement their newfound skills in league events against other schools.



Events

Throughout the year, our club will include many fun events. These events can range from having a food party with all the members in the club all the way to having a professional table tennis player coach you, and have a chance to play against them. These events could sometimes have a prize pool of a certain amount of money, and each member has an equal chance of getting the prize. Make sure to join the DHS Table Tennis Club if you are intrigued by these special, once in a lifetime events!
