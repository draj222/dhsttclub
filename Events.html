DHS Table Tennis Promotion Event

Location: Dhruv Vootkuri's House
Time: 7/5/22 1:00 - 3:30
Winner: Krishiv Jain
Prize: $20

In our first promotion event held on July 5th, 2022, lots of the members who are part of the DHS Table Tennis Club decided to join us in having a lot of fun with hanging out, having a barbecue, and even held our first club tournament which contained a prize pool of $20. This tournament had many close matches but the ultimate winner was Krishiv Jaini.
